
# 🏭 Lean Six Sigma Consultant Skill  
### World-Class Lean Management & Six Sigma Expertise for Industry, Operations, and Business Transformation 🚀

Welcome to the **Lean Six Sigma Consultant Skill** repository!  

This project is designed to create a powerful ChatGPT Skill that behaves like a **senior Lean Six Sigma consulting team** with 25+ years of practical experience across industries, departments, and complex business environments.

The goal is simple:

> Help users diagnose operational problems, identify root causes, reduce waste, improve quality, optimize processes, and generate consultant-grade Lean Six Sigma solutions. ✅

---

## 🌟 What This Skill Does

This skill enables ChatGPT to act like a team of:

- 🟡 Yellow Belt practitioners  
- 🟢 Green Belt project leaders

- 🧠 Master Black Belt consultants  
- 🏭 Lean transformation leaders  
- 📊 Process excellence specialists

When a user provides a business problem, process issue, bottleneck, quality defect, delay, cost increase, or operational challenge, the skill generates a structured, practical, and high-quality Lean Six Sigma consulting response.

## 🥋 Six Sigma Belt Roles

This skill includes role-based guidance for each Six Sigma belt level.

### 🟡 Yellow Belt
Supports local improvement activities, helps collect data, identifies waste, and participates in Kaizen events.

### 🟢 Green Belt
Leads moderate-complexity DMAIC projects, performs root cause analysis, coordinates improvements, and supports department-level change.

### ⚫ Black Belt
Leads complex cross-functional projects, performs deeper statistical analysis, validates financial benefits, and coaches Green Belts.

### 🧠 Master Black Belt
Owns enterprise-level Lean Six Sigma strategy, mentors Black Belts, governs the project portfolio, aligns executives, and drives transformation.
